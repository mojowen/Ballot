How Do I Vote?

What You’ve Gotta Bring

To avoid issues on Election Day, or if you are a first time voter, we recommend you bring some identification: Illinois driver’s license/state ID, utility bill, bank statement, or current college ID card.

If you are not already registered to vote, you may use Grace Period Voter Registration at early voting sites from March 11th - 22nd. Go to chicagoelections.com to find a location near you.

When & Where You’ve Gotta Go

Early voting is March 23rd through April 4th, and Election Day is April 7th. To find polling locations for Election Day or early voting go to chicagoelections.com

Alderman

Aldermen are legislators and members of the City Council who serve a four year term to represent the residents of an area of the city known as a ward. By custom, an alderman is a source of information and intermediary on behalf of their constituency about city functions and services within the ward.
