How Do I Vote?

What You’ve Gotta Bring

To avoid issues on Election Day, or if you are a first time voter, we recommend you bring some identification: Illinois driver’s license/state ID, utility bill, bank statement, or current college ID card.

If you are not already registered to vote, you may use Grace Period Voter Registration at early voting sites from February 17 - March 15. Go to chicagoelections.com to find a location near you.

When & Where You’ve Gotta Go

Early voting is February 29th through March 14th, and Election Day is Tuesday, March 15th. To find polling locations for Election Day or early voting go to chicagoelections.com
